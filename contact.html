❓ FAQ

Quick Answers

🎓

Is the CGPA calculator accurate?

Yes! It uses the official Superior University grading scale (A = 85%+, GP 4.00) exactly as per their academic regulations.

🖼️

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No! Image compression happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.

📝

Can I add my own blog posts?

Yes! Go to the Blog page, enable Admin Mode, and fill in the form. Posts are saved in your browser's local storage.

🆓

Is StudentPro free to use?

Completely free! No login, no sign-up, no ads. Just open and use — that's how it should be for students.
